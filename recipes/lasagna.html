Homemade Lasagna


This is my mom's special homemage lasagna recipe.

A special lasagna with a meaty, made-from-scratch tomato sauce and a deliciously cheesy filling. Serve with a leafy green salad and garlic bread.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
  • 2 teaspoons chopped fresh oregano
  • 1/8 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups grated mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Combine pork and ground beef in a large, deep skillet over medium-high heat. Cook until browned.
  2. Add onion and cook until translucent.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, garlic, basil, salt, oregano, and sugar. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer, stirring occasionally, for 30 minutes.
  4. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to boil. Cook lasagna noodles in water, stirring occasionally, until al dente. Drain and set aside. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  5. Mix ricotta cheese, parmesan cheese, mozarella cheese, and remaining parsley.
  6. Aseemble lasagna: Spread sauce on the bottom of a baking dish to coat it. Place a layer of noodles over the sauce. Layer with half of the cheese mixture and half of the remaining sauce. Repeat layers. Cover the dish with aluminum foil.
  7. Bake in preheated oven for 30 to 40 minutes. Remove the foil and bake until cheese is golden brown.
  8. Remove from the oven and let stand for 10 minutes before cutting and serving.
