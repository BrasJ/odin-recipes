Chicken Marsala


Quick and easy for a weeknight dinner and sophisticated enough for company.

Chicken Marsala is an Italian-style recipe for tender pan-friend chicken breasts in a sweet Marsala wine and mushroom sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 chopped fresh oregano
  • 4 medium skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - pounded 1/4 inch thick
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cupe sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup Marsala wine
  • 1/4 cup cooking sherry

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix together flour, salt, pepper, and oregano.
  2. Coat chicken pieces in flour mixture.
  3. In a large skillet, melt butter in olive oil over medium heat. Place chicken in the pan, and lightly brown.
  4. Flip the chicken pieces and add mushrooms. Pour in wine and sherry.
  5. Cover skillet; simmer chicken 10 minutes, turning once, until juices run clear.
  6. Serve hot an enjoy!
