Perfect Roast Chicken


The perfect recipe for a juicy roasted chicken.

Seasoned with a simple blend of herbs, this easy recipe will ensure a juicy chicken that is quick and easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 tablespoon garlic salt
  • 1 teaspoon coarsely ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme
  • 1 teaspoon fresh parsley
  • 1 teaspoon fresh rosemary

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Pat chicken dry with paper towels
  3. Combine butter, garlic salt, pepper, thyme, parsley, and rosemary. Thoroughly rub outside of chicken with butter mixture. Transfer to a baking dish.
  4. Bake in preheated oven until browned and juices run clear.
